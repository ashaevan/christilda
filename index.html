Our Products

Rotating Bobbin Holder

Our Christilda Bobbin Holders consist of anti-friction, thrust bearing, balls Separated, fluff protected, Specially individual tuned, built-in brake, fully dust proof, single action system. In our design, our Bobbin Holders will rotate freely (smooth run) and 100% guaranteed for the locking and unlocking system of roving Bobbin. This single action Bobbin Holders gives better and uniform yarn.

Non Rotating Bobbin Holder

Christilda Non Rotating Bobbin Holders can swing(tilt) as rotating Bobbin Holders and creeling and decreeling hitting will not cause any damage to the Bobbin Holders.

M19 Spindle Steel Pivot Finger Brake

Christilda Finger Brake is designed in steel pin, to with stand the brake applying load and its life is more. The existing finger brake plastic pivoted pin is not with standing the load applied on it.
